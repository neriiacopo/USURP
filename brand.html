Copywriting — rules at a glance
→ Declarative. Imperative. Second-person-singular.
→ Seize, never "get". Forfeit, never "lose". Uphold, never "show".
→ No contractions. you're → you are.
→ 8 words max on a button. 14 max on a modal header.
→ No emoji, ever. Glyphs only, in 1.5px stroke SVG.
→ Numerals tabular, zero-padded to 2: ◐ 07 — not 7.
